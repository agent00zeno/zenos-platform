## Preamble

ZenOS is a Business Operating Platform. Its purpose is to compress the time and cost required to launch and operate a real business — from months to days — by composing reusable capabilities into complete, governed business systems.

This Constitution governs the architecture, development, and operation of the ZenOS platform. It does not change frequently. When it does change, the change is deliberate and versioned.

No platform decision that conflicts with this Constitution is valid.

## What ZenOS Is

ZenOS is a platform that takes a business intent and produces a running business.

Input: "I want to start a construction company."  
Output: A running business — website, CRM, portals, AI agents, infrastructure, security, compliance — composed from reusable platform capabilities.

ZenOS is not:
- A website builder
- A SaaS product
- A consulting firm
- A collection of tools

ZenOS is the engine underneath all of those things.

---

## What ZenOS Is Not Yet

The following are on the roadmap but are not part of Phase 1:

- Cross-border bridge (US/China)
- Crypto/blockchain payment rails
- Marketplace (third-party agents and packs)
- Enterprise compliance planets
- ZENON governance layer (full implementation)
- Node.js backend services

Phase 1 proves one thing: **ZenOS can generate and operate a real business.** That business is Albany Zeno. AlbanyZeno.com is User #1.

---

## The Seven Capability Layers

Every product built on ZenOS is composed from these layers:

1. **Experience** — UI, mobile, voice
2. **Business** — workflows, rules, processes
3. **Intelligence** — AI agents and reasoning
4. **Automation** — orchestration and jobs
5. **Platform** — identity, APIs, data, messaging
6. **Infrastructure** — containers, compute, storage, networking
7. **Governance** — security, observability, compliance, cost

## Phase 1 Scope

**What ships in Phase 1:**
- `albanyzeno.com` live on ZenOS (`experience/public/`)
- ZenOS Alpha waitlist with business qualification intake
- Firebase as data layer (Firestore)
- Vite + React as the experience layer
- Vercel as deployment

**Definition of Done — Phase 1:**
- albanyzeno.com is live and running on ZenOS architecture
- Waitlist form captures name, email, business type, stage, needs
- At least one real visitor has submitted the form
- The Architect's Chair (`experience/architect-chair/`) is ready to begin

**What does not ship in Phase 1:**
- Everything else
